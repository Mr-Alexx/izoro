    Help

    • How far back can I search for items?

      Terapeak provides up to 1 year of historic sales data and is current up to the most recent hour.

    • I'm not able to find any results for my search, but there are active listings on eBay

      Terapeak Product research only displays listings that have sold items. It's possible that the active listings you see on the eBay site have no completed sales yet.

      If you're searching for eBay Motors listings, you have to select eBay Motors from the site selections in Terapeak Product Research first.

    • My search results say "Partial data shown." What does this mean?

      Terapeak Product Research is able to retrieve a maximum of 250,000 individual transactions per number of days included in your search.

      If your search exceeds 250,000 items in a single day and you would like to retrieve all relevant transactions related to your search, we recommend adding additional search terms or filters to further refine the data set.

    • How do I refine my keyword searches?

      If you search for multiple words, for example "iPhone XS Max 64GB", you'll see listings with all of these keywords (iPhone + XS + Max + 64GB) in the title.

      You can exclude results by adding "AND NOT" or "-" in front of a keyword. For example: "iPhone XS Max 64GB -cover" will exclude listings that have "cover" in the title.

      If you add "OR" between keywords, for example: iPhone XS MAX AND (64GB OR 256GB), this will show iPhone XS MAX listings with either 64GB OR 256GB in the title.

    • How do I search by Product ID?

      After you enter the Product ID, select AND or OR from the drop-down beside the Product ID field.

      If you select "AND", Terapeak will retrieve listings that include both the title keywords and the Product ID entered. If you select "OR", Terapeak will retrieve listings that match either your title keyword search, or the Product ID (some listings may not contain matching keywords, and some may not contain the Product ID.)

    • How do I use filters?

      You can add filters to your search including: Price, Listing format, Product Condition and Buyer's/Seller's Country.

      When you combine filters from each section, you'll get results that match the combined selections. For example, if you define the Price, Listing format and Product Condition the results will only include listings that represent the combination of all of these parameters.

      If you include multiple filters from the same section e.g. "listing format", we will look for listings that match any of those criteria. For example, if you look for both "Fixed price" and "Auction" listing formats, we will retrieve listings that are either "fixed price" OR "auction".
